Our Mission

We hope to create a website that is accessibility to everyone, so everyone can contribute to ending world poverty. Below is a table of some barriers that some of us face, and what the web developer of this site has done to address the issues.

Barrier Challenges What we did
Auditory Some content such as videos can pose a challenge if there are no subtitles or transcripts available. We made sure all of our content can be accessed through reading without sound.
Visual It can be hard to see colors that have a low color contrast, and smaller font can also be hard to read. We used an accessibility checker to make sure our color contrast passes the regulations.
Physical A number of challenges can occur, such as unable to click small links and having a hard time with navigation menus. We made sure our site has a simple navigation, and that background for the links in the navigation menu change color.
Cognitive/
Neurological 		Complex menu layouts can create problems with navigation and long paragraphs without pictures. We made sure to include pictures and to make our navigation simple.
We would love to hear back from you on the accessibility and user friendliness of our site. You can click here to leave feedback.
